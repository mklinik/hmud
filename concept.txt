- there are rooms, items and characters
- every room, object and player has a description
- the description of items is static
- the description of rooms is a static text plus the list characters plus the list of items
- the description of characters depends on equipment and stats
- every player has:
  - stats
  - an inventory (with objects)
  - equipped objects
- stats are:
  - level
  - gender
  - race (Human, Elven, Dwarf)
  - role (depends on gender, e.g. King/Queen)
